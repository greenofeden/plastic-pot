Why Choose Us

WHAT

Recycling plant pots and other types of containers with our solution helps to preserve natural resources, lower energy use, and limit trash. By using our recyclable plant pots, people and organizations may lessen the quantity of garbage that is disposed of in landfills and incinerators and help create a society that is more ecologically conscious and sustainable.

WHY

In order to reduce waste, save money, and create a more sustainable and ecologically friendly approach to grow plants, we want to pursue the usage of recyclable plant pots. This was an idea that we had in mind since plastics live longer and are more durable than clay plant pots.

HOW

People and businesses can reduce clutter in Metro Manila, save money, and contribute to a more sustainable and environmentally friendly society by using our product, "recyclable plant pots," and participating in recycling programs. The revenue of our product depends on its size; we have 3 sizes, which are small ₱40, medium ₱120, and large ₱200.
